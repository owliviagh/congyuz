The KOWL NFT App Design

NFT is an abbreviation for Non-Fungible Token. It is a type of digital asset that represents ownership of a unique item or piece of content, like a painting, video, music album, or even a social media post. Unlike fungible tokens, such as Bitcoin, which are interchangeable, each NFT is unique and cannot be exchanged for another NFT. Typically, NFTs are stored on a blockchain, which ensures secure and transparent verification of ownership and transfer of the digital asset. The popularity of NFTs has surged in recent years as a new way of selling and buying digital assets, with some NFTs selling for millions of dollars.

Due to the high demand for NFTs, I came up with the idea of creating an app for this purpose. I named it “KOWL”.

The subject of social connection is fascinating because it deals with the intricate bonds among individuals, and it is a multifaceted yet tangible idea that continuously offers fresh possibilities.

The exchange of information between people has become so vast that social products have emerged to cater to our needs. With the pandemic driving a need for immersive digital interaction, the emergence of the metaverse into our daily lives is inevitable and adoption rates are likely to soar.

Nonetheless, it is vital to steer clear of constructing the metaverse using outdated centralized Web2.0 technology. This technology is characterized by bots, obscure algorithms, and proprietary protocols that produce silos and restrict the circulation of values. Relying on such technology may result in a scenario similar to that depicted in "Ready Player One," where a dominant service provider can manipulate perceptions for their own advantage.

Figma

Information Structure:

To create a well-organized and comprehensive interface for the app, I utilized a structured approach.

Splash Screens:

The initial screens that a user sees when they open the app for the first time.

Sign up: Easy to switch between sign-up and log-in page. The app uses grayed-out text to assist users in identifying which information needs to be filled out.

Discover: Users can explore communities freely by interests or areas on the app. They can also follow the latest updates on Web3.0 from Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) and artists. Furthermore, they can access the market page through the links shared.

Message: Users can communicate with other people in the community through the messaging feature in the app. Blue bubbles are designed to indicate notifications.

Create: Users can create their own NFT collections and share or sell them on the platform. Alternatively, they can also create DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) and write articles in the app.

Market: Users can browse the marketplace, view the hottest collections and creators, and buy or sell NFTs in the app.

User Profile: Users can manage their blockchain wallets and profiles.

The Vision: KOWL is an NFT social app designed for artists that prioritizes diversity and inclusivity.

Its key features include:

• A social networking platform for Web3.0 that is designed to be diverse and inclusive.

• A fair and open metaverse that is accessible to anyone, anytime, and anywhere.

• Recognition of the shared experiences of diverse social groups while also celebrating their differences.

• Upholding the independence of individuals as crucial to achieving data and value freedom.

• A truly decentralized governance system, where both objective and subjective governance are prioritized.

• An open and accessible platform for artists and creators from all corners of the world.
